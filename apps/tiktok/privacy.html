Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-03-01

At TokDL, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use TokDL, we collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • TikTok video URLs that you submit for downloading
  • Any information you voluntarily provide when contacting our support team

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage:

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent)
  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Usage patterns and preferences

1.3 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. These technologies help us understand how you use our service, remember your preferences, and improve our offerings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Service Provision

To provide and maintain our TikTok video downloading service

Analytics

To understand usage patterns and improve our service

Troubleshooting

To diagnose problems and optimize performance

Security

To detect and prevent fraudulent activity and security incidents

3. Video Processing and Storage

3.1 Video Processing

When you submit a TikTok URL for downloading:

  • We temporarily process the video on our servers to remove watermarks and prepare it for download
  • The content is not reviewed by our team unless necessary to diagnose technical issues

3.2 Storage Policy

TokDL does not permanently store the videos you download. After processing:

  • Processed videos are automatically deleted from our servers after download or within 24 hours
  • We do not maintain a database of downloaded videos or associate downloads with individual users

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).
